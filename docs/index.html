🏆 Grand Old Lady’s Contest

A celebration of creativity, tradition, and innovation.

About the Contest

The Grand Old Lady’s Contest (GOLC) invites creators, thinkers, and innovators to reinterpret legacy through modern storytelling or design.

Contest Categories

  • 360 — for location
  • snapath — for path, trajectory, or journey
  • daysnap — for time
  • DADSON — for family
  • Creative — for anything else creative

How to Participate

  1. Fork the repository.
  2. Add your submission under the submissions/ folder.
  3. Submit a pull request before the deadline.
